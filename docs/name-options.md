# Naming Options

This project does not need a personal or purely descriptive name forever.

If the goal is to make it feel stronger for recruiters, collaborators, or future productization, a more commercial name may help.

## Best Balanced Options

### 1. `SignalForge`

Why it works:

- sounds product-like
- suggests shaping a clear professional signal
- broad enough to grow beyond one use case

Tradeoff:

- less immediately personal than `DavidSan`

### 2. `ProfilePilot`

Why it works:

- easy to understand quickly
- directly connected to professional positioning
- recruiter-friendly and legible

Tradeoff:

- slightly more generic

### 3. `Narra`

Why it works:

- short and brandable
- connected to narrative and self-expression
- easy to remember

Tradeoff:

- less explicit about the professional use case

### 4. `SignalPath`

Why it works:

- sounds like a product
- implies direction, clarity, and communication
- still flexible if the project expands

Tradeoff:

- more abstract than descriptive

### 5. `ValueDraft`

Why it works:

- clearly tied to articulating value
- feels practical, not academic
- good fit for an AI writing assistant

Tradeoff:

- narrower than a broader agent platform name

## Best Option If You Want To Keep Personal Identity

### `DavidSan AI`

Why it works:

- preserves authorship and personal origin
- sounds more product-like than just `DavidSan`
- easy transition from the current public repo

Tradeoff:

- less scalable as a standalone brand if you want distance from your personal name later

## My Recommendation

If the main goal is recruiter appeal plus future product credibility:

- best brand-style option: `SignalForge`
- best clear/professional option: `ProfilePilot`
- best low-friction evolution from current name: `DavidSan AI`
